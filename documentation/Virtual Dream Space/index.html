Floating Island Dream

Description

Space Description: A small island floats in the sky, surrounded by clouds. Trees, a house, and glowing crystals make it feel magical and dreamy. Stars are scattered for the player to collect.

Interactions:

  • Touch the crystal → the island slowly rises, clouds move, and a sound plays.
  • Jump on the clouds → the player bounces higher using physics.
  • Collect stars → each star disappears, and after collecting all, a rainbow gate appears.

Sketch

Clouds are bounce platforms
Stars are collectibles
Crystal triggers special interaction
Rainbow Gate appears after collecting stars

Interactive Elements

Player Movement: The player can walk around the island using a first-person controller.

Clouds: Act as bouncing platforms. Jumping on them propels the player upward.

Stars: Collectible objects that disappear when touched, increasing the star count.

Crystal: Triggers a special interaction, launching the player upward into space.

Rainbow Gate: Appears after collecting all five stars. Entering the gate shows a victory message with music and particles.

Development Process

  1. Scene Setup: Created floating island environment with dark space skybox.
  2. Player Movement: Implemented first-person controller.
  3. Environment Objects: Added house, trees, clouds, stars, and crystal.
  4. Interactive Systems: Clouds bounce, stars collectible, crystal launches player.
  5. Game Goal: Added rainbow gate after collecting stars.
  6. Feedback Effects: Music, particle effects, and victory message at the end.
