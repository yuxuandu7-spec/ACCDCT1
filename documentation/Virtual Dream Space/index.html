Floating Island Dream

Description

Space Description: A small island floats in the sky, surrounded by clouds. Trees, a house, and glowing crystals make it feel magical and dreamy. Stars are scattered for the player to collect. Interactions: Touch the crystal → the island slowly rises, clouds move, and a sound plays. Jump on the clouds → the player bounces higher using physics. Collect stars → each star disappears, and after collecting all, a rainbow gate appears.
